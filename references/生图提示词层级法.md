# 生图提示词层级法

This reference distills the HuntPrompts AI image prompt guides into a reusable asset-prompt workflow for the Seedance storyboard skill.

## Core Principle

High-control image prompts should be built in layers instead of as a loose pile of adjectives. For storyboard assets, use this order:

```text
主体/动作 -> 环境/时空 -> 光影/材质/色彩 -> 风格/媒介 -> 镜头/构图 -> 技术规格/负面约束
```

This keeps the image model clear on what must remain stable, what can vary, and what aesthetic system should govern the final image.

## Layer 1: 主体

Use this layer to define the image anchor.

- 类型：人物、群像、动物、建筑、道具、怪物、车辆、符号。
- 数量：单人、双人、三人、群众、队列、对称构图。
- 动作：站立、回头、奔跑、拔剑、推门、凝视、哭泣、拥抱、攻击、跌倒。
- 造型：年龄段、身形、脸型、发型、服装剪影、配饰、妆容、伤痕。
- 神态：冷静、惊惧、愤怒、克制、疲惫、坚定、空洞、温柔。
- 身份符号：职业、阶层、阵营、时代、宗教/门派/家族纹样、代表色、随身物。

Character consistency checklist:

- Give every major character one silhouette feature, one color marker, and one object marker.
- Keep face, hair, costume, and body proportions stable across asset prompts.
- Change only pose, expression, shot size, or scene when generating variants.

## Layer 2: 环境

Use this layer to make the picture physically and narratively believable.

- 空间类型：宫殿、废墟、村落、街巷、山门、客栈、办公室、地铁、审讯室、荒原。
- 时间天气：黎明、正午、黄昏、深夜、雨、雪、雾、沙尘、闷热、台风前。
- 空间结构：前景遮挡、中景行动区、远景地标、纵深走廊、门窗、台阶、水面。
- 材质：湿石板、粗布、金属锈蚀、漆木、玻璃反射、纸张纤维、尘埃、烟雾。
- 色彩：冷青、暖金、红黑、灰白、低饱和、单色、互补色、主色加点缀色。

Scene consistency checklist:

- Name the light source and where it comes from.
- Include at least one depth cue for large scenes.
- Include texture or surface words for tactile realism.

## Layer 3: 风格

Choose a style family that matches the story genre and production goal.

Short-drama useful families:

- 电影写实：photoreal, cinematic, 35mm film, shallow depth of field, film grain.
- 国风水墨：traditional Chinese ink painting, ink wash, negative space, misty mountains, flowing brushwork.
- 仙侠动漫：中国仙侠、飘逸衣衫、云雾山水、灵力光效、东方奇幻。
- 复古港风：80s/90s Hong Kong film look, neon signs, humid night, tungsten interior light.
- 悬疑犯罪：low key, hard shadows, rain-slick street, handheld documentary feeling.
- 赛博都市：cyberpunk, neon reflections, wet pavement, holographic signs, high-tech low-life.
- 3D动画：Pixar/Disney-like 3D, soft materials, expressive faces, volumetric light.
- 角色概念设计：character concept art, design sheet, callouts, front/side/rear view.
- 产品/道具图：product shot, clean background, rim light, material detail, orthographic view.

Style selection rules:

- Use one dominant style anchor per project.
- Use sub-style tags only when they clarify a scene, such as `90s retro anime` for flashback or `ink illustration` for legend narration.
- Avoid mixing too many named artists or franchise styles in one prompt; prefer descriptive style traits.

## Layer 4: 光影

Use light to drive emotion and readability.

Natural light:

- 黄金时刻：warm golden sunlight, long shadows, romantic warmth.
- 蓝调时刻：cool twilight, deep blue sky, quiet mystery.
- 阴天漫射：soft even light, muted tones, gentle realism.
- 斑驳光：leaf-filtered light, moving patches, nostalgic softness.

Studio light:

- 伦勃朗光：45-degree side light, triangular cheek highlight, dramatic portrait.
- 蝴蝶光：front-above beauty lighting, clean cheekbones, fashion or royal portrait.
- 分割光：half face in shadow, moral conflict, mystery.
- 边缘光：back rim light, clear silhouette, heroic separation.
- 高调光/低调光：bright optimistic clarity vs. shadow-heavy suspense.

Cinematic and special light:

- 体积光：visible beams through dust, fog, or incense.
- 电影光：layered lighting, motivated sources, dramatic contrast.
- 霓虹光：colored reflections, urban night, cyberpunk mood.
- 宽银幕光晕：horizontal lens flare, industrial sci-fi.
- 生物发光：organic blue-green glow for fantasy, cave, monster, or underwater scenes.
- 焦散光：water/glass refraction patterns for pools, underwater, dream sequences.
- 英雄光/反派光：back halo and low angle vs. underlight/green sickly glow.

Lighting formula:

```text
[主体] + [环境] + [核心灯光术语] + [冷暖/明暗/反射/体积感] + [情绪词]
```

## Asset Templates

### Character

```text
[统一风格前缀]，[角色身份、年龄段、体型、脸型、发型、服装剪影、标志色、标志物]，[姿态和基准表情]，[所处核心环境或干净背景]，[主光源、轮廓光、主色调]，[半身/全身/三视图/特写]，[清晰五官，稳定角色设计，9:16或1:1]
```

### Scene

```text
[统一风格前缀]，[地点类型、时代地域、空间布局]，[前景/中景/远景元素]，[时间、天气、空气、材质表面]，[核心灯光、色彩、景深]，[广角/鸟瞰/低机位/电影构图]，[无角色或环境参考图]
```

### Prop

```text
[统一风格前缀]，[道具形状、尺寸、材质、磨损、纹样、功能结构]，[纯色背景或剧情场景]，[产品照/正侧后视图/极端特写]，[柔和棚拍光或剧情光源]，[清晰轮廓，易复用]
```

## Prompt QA

- Does the prompt state the subject before modifiers?
- Does the environment include space, time, and material texture?
- Is the light source named and emotionally matched to the scene?
- Is the style anchor specific enough to prevent generic AI-image output?
- Are character identity markers stable across episodes?
- Are aspect ratio, shot size, and intended use included?
- Are conflicting style or lighting instructions removed?
